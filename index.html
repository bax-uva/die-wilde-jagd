Die Wilde Jagd (“The Wild Hunt”) is the music project of Berlin-based producer and songwriter Sebastian Lee Philipp, in collaboration with co-producer Ralf Beck and several guest musicians. The eponymous debut album – a dense, atmospheric mix of guitar loops, electronic percussion, drums, synthesizers and German lyrics – was released in 2015. After recording the “Geisterfahrer” EP for Correspondant Records in 2017, Die Wilde Jagd’s sophomore album “Uhrwald Orange” was released in 2018 on Bureau B. to much critical acclaim. The Die Wilde Jagd two piece Live band (consisting of Sebastian Lee Philipp on guitar, electronics and vocals with Ran Levari on drums) are regularly touring Europe and beyond, playing a selection of Live versions of the band’s releases.
